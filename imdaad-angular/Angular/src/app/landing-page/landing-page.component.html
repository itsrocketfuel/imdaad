About Imdaad

Imdaad isn't a person, group, or company. Imdaad is you.
It is a non-profit, collect and deliver service that helps circulate unused resources from people
and helps successfully deliver those resources to those in need.

Donate Medicine

It is common in households to have medicine lying around that is going to waste. There could be medicine in your own household as well that may not be useful for you, but it will mean a good health for others. Donate them now!

Donate Clothes

We're sure that you must have some extra piece of clothing lying around in your house, or hanging in your wardrobe for months and years. Let us help you save yourself some space. Deliver those clothes now!

Donate Food

It's extremely common that in an average household, restaurants or any other eating place, enough food is left over that it can feed some mouths. When you're done eating and you notice some food left over, please pack it ready to be delivered and help some hungry stomachs!
